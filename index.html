Who I am

My journey into engineering ignited very early. I discovered how much I enjoyed tinkering and creating during middle school. I was very fortunate to attend a school with an early engineering program, a blessing that blossomed my passion. My enjoyment with the subject only grew from there. I immersed myself in courses, modeling intricate parts in CAD and working on my very own robot! With a clear vision of my future, I pursued this passion into college, driven by an ambition to achieve more. Along this path, I've had the privilege of contributing to multiple robot projects, sharing my knowledge through teaching, and collaborating with none other than NASA! The excitement for the future propels me forward, and I am eager to share this enthusiasm with you.
